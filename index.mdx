SaveMate is the universal video stream detector for your browser. It automatically identifies HLS, MP4, and WebM streams on any website, letting you download, convert, cast, and organize your video content.

## What can SaveMate do?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stream Detection" icon="signal-stream" href="/features/stream-detection">
    Automatically detects HLS (m3u8), MP4, and WebM streams as you browse. The SaveMate icon lights up when a stream is found.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Video Downloads" icon="download" href="/features/video-downloads">
    Download videos in qualities up to 4K and 8K. Choose your preferred format and quality before saving.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Format Conversion" icon="arrows-rotate" href="/features/format-conversion">
    Convert HLS/m3u8 streams to MP4, MKV, MOV, or WebM. Extract audio to MP3 or AAC.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chromecast" icon="tv" href="/features/chromecast">
    Cast detected streams directly to your Chromecast or AirPlay-compatible devices.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cloud Storage" icon="cloud-arrow-up" href="/features/cloud-storage">
    Sync downloads to Google Drive, Dropbox, AWS S3, or Cloudflare R2 automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Video Library" icon="folder-open" href="/features/video-library">
    Save and organize videos in your cloud library. Access them from any device.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the extension">
    Add SaveMate to Chrome, Edge, or Brave from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/lejgkageoahacjmljeogapbkhbfldigd). Takes less than 10 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse any video site">
    Visit a website with video content. The SaveMate icon in your toolbar lights up when a stream is detected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or cast">
    Click the icon to download, convert, or cast the video. Choose your preferred quality and format.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Free vs Professional

SaveMate is free to install and use. The free plan includes standard quality downloads (up to 720p), 3 HD downloads per day, and storage for 10 videos in your cloud library.

Upgrade to Professional for unlimited 4K/8K downloads, cloud sync, format conversion, and an unlimited video library.
