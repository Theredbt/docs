## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is SaveMate really free?">
    Yes. SaveMate's free plan includes unlimited standard quality downloads (up to 720p), 3 HD downloads per day, Chromecast support, and storage for 10 videos in your cloud library. No credit card required.

    The Professional plan unlocks unlimited 4K/8K downloads, cloud sync, format conversion, and an unlimited video library.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SaveMate work on Mac and Windows?">
    Yes. SaveMate works on macOS, Windows, and Linux — any platform that runs a supported Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, or Brave).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it legal and safe?">
    SaveMate is designed for personal archiving and fair use. The extension is verified on the Chrome Web Store and reviewed by Google's security team. All video processing happens locally in your browser — no data is collected, transmitted, or tracked.

    Always respect content creators' rights and the terms of service of the websites you visit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SaveMate work on mobile?">
    Browser extensions are a desktop-only feature, so SaveMate cannot be installed on mobile browsers. However, if you have a SaveMate account with cloud library enabled, you can access your saved videos from any device through the SaveMate web app.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Downloads

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I download a video?">
    1. Install the SaveMate extension from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/lejgkageoahacjmljeogapbkhbfldigd)
    2. Navigate to any page with video content
    3. When the SaveMate icon lights up, click it
    4. Select your preferred quality and format
    5. Click Download

    See the [Quick Start guide](/quickstart) for a step-by-step walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What video sites are supported?">
    SaveMate works with any website that uses HLS (m3u8), MP4, or WebM streaming. This covers the vast majority of video hosting and streaming platforms on the web.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if a download fails?">
    SaveMate includes retry mechanisms for unstable connections. For HLS streams, downloads can be resumed from where they left off. If issues persist, try:

    1. Refreshing the page and re-detecting the stream
    2. Checking your internet connection
    3. Trying a different quality level
    4. Contacting support if the issue continues
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why am I limited to 720p?">
    Free users can download unlimited videos at 720p and below. Downloads at 1080p and above are limited to 3 per day on the free plan. [Upgrade to Professional](/billing/plans) for unlimited high-quality downloads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Extension

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which browsers are supported?">
    SaveMate supports all Chromium-based browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Brave. Firefox support is coming soon. The extension works on macOS, Windows, and Linux.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update the extension?">
    Chrome, Edge, and Brave automatically update extensions in the background. You'll always have the latest version without any manual steps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the extension safe?">
    Yes. SaveMate is verified on the Chrome Web Store and reviewed by Google's security team. The extension requests minimal permissions and all video processing happens locally in your browser. No browsing data is collected or transmitted.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Lifetime Access work?">
    Lifetime Access is a one-time payment that gives you permanent access to all Professional features, including all future updates. There are no recurring charges and no expiration date. Learn more on the [Lifetime Access page](/billing/lifetime).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription?">
    Yes, you can cancel anytime from your [billing dashboard](https://savemate.io/billing). You'll retain access to Professional features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    SaveMate accepts all major credit and debit cards through Stripe, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and more.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
