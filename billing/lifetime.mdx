SaveMate Lifetime Access is a one-time purchase that gives you permanent access to all Professional features, including all future updates.

## What's included

Lifetime Access includes everything in the Professional plan:

- Unlimited downloads at all qualities (4K, 8K, 60fps)
- Unlimited cloud library storage
- Cloud sync to all supported providers
- Format conversion and audio extraction
- Cross-device sync
- Priority support
- All future feature releases

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase lifetime access">
    Visit [savemate.io/pricing](https://savemate.io/pricing) and select the **Lifetime** option. Complete the one-time payment through Stripe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant activation">
    Your account is immediately upgraded to Professional with lifetime access. No recurring charges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use forever">
    Your lifetime access never expires. You'll continue to receive all new features and updates at no additional cost.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upgrading from a subscription

If you have an active monthly or yearly subscription, purchasing lifetime access will:

1. Activate your lifetime access immediately
2. Cancel your recurring subscription automatically
3. Apply credit for any unused subscription time toward the lifetime purchase

You won't be double-charged, and the transition is seamless.
