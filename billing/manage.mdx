Manage your SaveMate subscription from the billing dashboard at [savemate.io/billing](https://savemate.io/billing).

## Billing dashboard

The billing page shows your complete subscription overview:

- **Current plan** — Your active plan name and status (Active, Canceling, Past Due)
- **Next billing date** — When your subscription renews
- **Payment methods** — Your saved cards and default payment method
- **Invoices** — Complete billing history with downloadable receipts

## Change your plan

### Upgrade

To upgrade from Free to Professional:

1. Go to [savemate.io/billing](https://savemate.io/billing)
2. Select your preferred plan (Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime)
3. Enter your payment details
4. Your upgrade takes effect immediately

### Switch intervals

To switch between monthly and yearly billing:

1. Go to your billing dashboard
2. Select the new billing interval
3. The change takes effect at your next billing date, with prorated credits applied

### Downgrade / Cancel

To cancel your Professional subscription:

1. Go to your billing dashboard
2. Click **Cancel subscription**
3. Your Professional features remain active until the end of your current billing period
4. After the billing period ends, your account reverts to the Free plan

<Tip>
  You can resume a cancelled subscription at any time before the billing period ends. Your access continues uninterrupted.
</Tip>

## Payment methods

### Add a payment method

1. Go to your billing dashboard
2. Click **Add payment method**
3. Enter your card details through the secure Stripe payment form
4. The new card is saved to your account

### Set a default method

If you have multiple payment methods, click the method you want to use as default. All future charges will use this card.

### Remove a payment method

Click the remove button next to any non-default payment method to delete it from your account.

## Invoices

View and download all past invoices from your billing dashboard. Each invoice includes:

- Invoice number and date
- Plan details and billing period
- Amount charged
- Payment method used
- Downloadable PDF receipt

## Coupons and discounts

If you have a coupon code, apply it during checkout or from your billing dashboard. Active discounts are shown on your plan details.
