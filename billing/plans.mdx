## Free plan details

The free plan is designed for casual users who want standard quality downloads:

- **Download quality**: Up to 720p (unlimited), 1080p+ (3 per day)
- **Cloud library**: Store up to 10 saved videos
- **Chromecast**: Full casting support included
- **No account required**: Basic downloads work without signing up

## Professional plan details

Professional unlocks everything SaveMate has to offer:

- **Unlimited downloads** at all qualities including 4K, 8K, and 60fps
- **Unlimited cloud library** with no cap on saved videos
- **Cloud sync** to Google Drive, Dropbox, AWS S3, and Cloudflare R2
- **Format conversion** from HLS to MP4, MKV, MOV, or WebM
- **Audio extraction** to MP3 or AAC
- **Cross-device sync** to access your library from any device
- **Priority support** for faster issue resolution
